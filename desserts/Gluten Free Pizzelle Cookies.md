*Notes*

- Yields ~33 cookies.
- Tends to produce less dainty cookies compared to standard pizzelle recipes.

### Ingredients

- 1 ¾ cups gluten free Bisquick
- 3 large eggs
- ¾ cup granulated sugar
- ½ cup melted butter
- 1 tbsp of vanilla extract
- 1/8 tsp of lemon extract
- 1 tbsp anise extract
- 1/2 tbsp anise seeds, crushed

### Instructions

1. Combine butter, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, lemon extract, anise extract, and anise seeds.

2. Add the Bisquick and using a rubber spatula stir everything together until just combined but don’t over mix.

3. Measure heaping tablespoons of batter into the Pizzelle maker and cook for 30 seconds each.

4. Let cool on cookie cooling racks.
